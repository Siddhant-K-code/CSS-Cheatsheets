Alignment In Flexbox. Auto margins

Before reading the cheatsheet you should look at the following graphic that I use to mark elements areas:

the flex-container area the flex item area

The flex-container has one item

When the flex-container has one item and we set the margin to auto then a browser will try to give as big space as it's possible in the direction to borders of the flex-container.

margin-left: auto (305px)
margin-bottom: auto (105px)
margin-left: auto (305px)
margin-top: auto (105px)
margin-right: auto (305px)
margin-top: auto (105px)

If the sizes of flex item equals or more of flex-container's sizes the margin will be set in 0.

margin-right: auto (0px)
margin-bottom: auto
(105px)
margin-right: auto (305px)
margin-bottom: auto (0px)

The flex-cotainer has more than one item

When the flex-container has more than one item and we set the margin to auto for them then a browser will try to give as big space as it's possible in the direction to borders of others items and if they're not then to borders of the flex-container.
